/Way

In this section I will tell you about my path to IT. It begins with the fact that in 2022 I was drafted into the army. During my service, I met a programmer and I began to show interest in his business, since I was far from the IT field. I was interested in how web applications, games, and websites are created using lines of code. He told me the basics, how everything is done using programming languages. And at the end of the service, I had an incredible desire to try to program something with my own hands.
I left the army at the end of 2023 and immediately began self-training. My training included reading articles on programming, studying documentation, watching video tutorials and practicing. I combined my studies with work as an energy engineer. Technologies that I study: Python, Javascript and their frameworks (Django, FastApi, Flask, React, Node, Vue), HTML, CSS, SQL.
I strive to become a full-stack developer and get a job in a company. To realize this goal, I began to practice intensively, implementing my mini pet projects. Oddly enough, programming has become my hobby, to which I devote at least 6-8 hours a day. I couldn’t even imagine that I would connect my life with IT. And now I can’t live without it.
